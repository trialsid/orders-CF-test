Topic: Caching and Performance

ETags are the “last checked” stamps; caches keep the line short.

Products (`functions/products.js`)
- Global ETag = active product count + max(updated_at) of products + max(updated_at) of admin_config.
- Cache: `public, max-age=60, stale-while-revalidate=600`.
- 304 supported; `useProducts` handles 304/AbortError gracefully.

Orders (`functions/order.js`)
- Per-order ETag on `id` lookups (weak ETag with updated_at).
- Customer-global ETag (count + max updated_at) when unfiltered/non-privileged; private cache headers on hits.
- Otherwise `Cache-Control: no-store`. `useOrders` honors 304 and revalidates on focus.

Users (`functions/_auth.js`, `functions/admin/users.js`)
- `/auth/me`: per-user ETag (updated_at/token_version).
- `/admin/users`: global ETag (count + max updated_at) with private cache headers; supports 304.

Client Storage
- LocalStorage only for theme (`order-ieeja-theme`), locale (`order-ieeja-locale`), cart (`order-ieeja-cart`), and checkout draft.
